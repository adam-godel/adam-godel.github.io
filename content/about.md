## About Me
I am a junior undergraduate studying mathematics and computer science at Boston University. While I am interested in a multitude of topics within my major, my main interests are quantum computing, distributed systems, and cryptography.

I am currently a researcher in the BU CASP Systems Lab focusing on secure multi-party computation as well as a WISER Quantum Solutions Launchpad (QSL) fellow affiliated with the US Naval Nuclear Lab focusing on benchmarking variational quantum algorithms. I have also worked on a handful of research projects for quantum computing companies as well as web projects for nonprofits.
